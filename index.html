My family has 4 pets. Lily is our dog, Luna our cat, and Vader & Sonic are our two turtles

Lily

Lily is a German Sheperd and is almost 10 year old. She loves to be petted and loves playing tug of war. She greets everyone with lots of kisses.

Luna

Luna is our newest pet, an orange Tabby to be specific. She loves attention even more than Lily, but she can be shy around new people

Vader

Vader is my red eared slider turtle and he is very lazy. He spends all of his time basking under his lamp and just like me, loves to eat.

Sonic

Sonic is much bigger compared to Vader and is very active. He loves his swim time and whenever he is out of his tank, he likes to run.
